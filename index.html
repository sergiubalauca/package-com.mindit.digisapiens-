DigiSapiens Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5th September 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DigiSapiens ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Audio Recording

Our app requests permission to access your device's microphone for the following purposes:

  • Reading Practice: To record your voice while reading exercises
  • Voice Exercises: To provide interactive voice-based learning activities
  • Speech Recognition: To analyze pronunciation and provide feedback

Data Collection and Storage

When you use audio features in our app:

  • Audio recordings are stored locally on your device
  • Some audio data may be temporarily processed on our secure servers for analysis
  • We do not share your audio recordings with third parties
  • Audio data is used solely to provide the app's educational functionality

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your audio recordings at any time through the app settings
  • Revoke microphone permission through your device settings
  • Contact us to request deletion of any stored data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your audio data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
